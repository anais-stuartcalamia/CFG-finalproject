Let's Play A Game!

The focus of this game is to finish the game through different choices. There are three endings to this game, one may be taken from popular science-fiction and the other could be more plausible.
You start of your journey as a successful resercher/analyst for a company that helps governemnts with providing them information on the latest scientiifc environmental research. You are the one in charge in analysing if the information is worth reporting or that it should need more research/information on the subject before handing it over. The game starts on a beautiful spring morning as you enter your office buidling and get ready for your work day.
